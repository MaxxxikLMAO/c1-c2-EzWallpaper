Forza Horizon

Forza Horizon is an open-world racing video game for Microsoft's Xbox video game console developed by Playground Games in association with Turn 10 Studios. The game is the fifth release under the Forza series, although it was (at the time of its release) a spin-off of the original Forza Motorsport title scheme.[2] Forza Horizon 2 was released in 2014 and Forza Horizon 3 in 2016; Forza Horizon 4 was released in October 2018.
